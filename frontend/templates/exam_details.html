COMEDK Exam Details

A complete guide to the Consortium of Medical, Engineering and Dental Colleges of Karnataka exam.

What is COMEDK?

COMEDK stands for Consortium of Medical, Engineering and Dental Colleges of Karnataka.

It is an autonomous body formed by private professional colleges in Karnataka to conduct a common entrance test for admissions.

Purpose of Exam

  • Provide fair and transparent admissions
  • Avoid multiple entrance tests
  • Admit students into private engineering colleges of Karnataka

Types of Exams

1. COMEDK UGET

For Undergraduate Engineering (B.E / B.Tech)

Main exam students write after 12th

2. COMEDK PGET

For Postgraduate Engineering (M.E / M.Tech)

Most students refer to COMEDK UGET when they say "COMEDK exam".

Eligibility Criteria (UGET)

Educational Qualification
  • Must have passed 10+2 / PU / equivalent
  • Subjects: Physics, Mathematics (mandatory), Chemistry / Biotechnology / Biology / CS / Electronics
  • Minimum marks:
    • 45% (General)
    • 40% (SC/ST/OBC – Karnataka)
Nationality
  • Indian citizens
  • OCI / PIO candidates also eligible

Syllabus

Based on PUC / CBSE / ISC Class 11 & 12 syllabus.

  • Physics: Laws of motion, optics, electricity
  • Chemistry: Organic, inorganic, physical
  • Mathematics: Algebra, calculus, trigonometry

Rank Generation

Rank is calculated based on Total marks.

Tie-breaking rules:
  1. Higher Mathematics marks
  2. Higher Physics marks
  3. Age (older candidate preferred)
